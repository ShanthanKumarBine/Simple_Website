iPhone 15 Pro Max

The iPhone 15 Pro Max is the largest and most powerful model in Apple's 2023 iPhone lineup.   1. Aerospace-grade titanium design: The iPhone 15 Pro Max is made from aerospace-grade titanium, which is both strong and lightweight.
  2. USB-C connector: The iPhone 15 Pro Max is the first iPhone to feature a USB-C connector, making it easier to charge and connect to other devices.
  3. All-day battery life: The iPhone 15 Pro Max has an all-day battery life, so you can use it all day long without having to worry about running out of power.
  4. 48MP main camera with multiple focal lengths: The iPhone 15 Pro Max has a new 48MP main camera with multiple focal lengths, giving you more flexibility when taking photos and videos.
  5. Telephoto lens with 5x optical zoom: The iPhone 15 Pro Max also has a telephoto lens with 5x optical zoom, so you can zoom in on distant objects without losing quality.
  6. Customizable Action button: The iPhone 15 Pro Max has a new customizable Action button that can be programmed to perform your favorite tasks, such as launching a camera mode or opening a specific app.
  7. A17 Pro chip: The iPhone 15 Pro Max is powered by the new A17 Pro chip, which is the fastest and most powerful chip ever in a smartphone.
  8. The iPhone 15 Pro Max is available in four colors: black titanium, white titanium, blue titanium, and natural titanium. It starts at $1,199 for the 256GB model and goes up to $1,599 for the 1TB model.
